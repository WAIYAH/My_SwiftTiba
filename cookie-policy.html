Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. SwiftTiba uses cookies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function correctly. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Purpose Duration
st_cookie_consent Records your cookie consent preference 365 days
st_dark_mode Remembers your light/dark theme preference 365 days

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Platform by collecting and reporting anonymous usage information.

Cookie Purpose Duration
Analytics identifiers Track page views, user sessions, and navigation patterns Up to 2 years

Analytics cookies are only set if you click "Accept All" on our cookie banner.

2.3 Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services may set cookies when you use features on our Platform:

  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet.js: For our hospital map feature. Tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap servers.
  • Google Fonts: For loading our Inter and Open Sans typefaces.
  • Font Awesome CDN: For icon delivery.

3. Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit SwiftTiba, you will see a cookie consent banner offering two options:

  • Accept All: Enables essential + analytics cookies
  • Essential Only: Only essential cookies are used (no analytics tracking)

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.
