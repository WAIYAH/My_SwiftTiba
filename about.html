About SwiftTiba

Empowering Healthcare Access with AI-Driven Solutions

We are dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare by providing instant access to hospital locations, first aid guidance, and AI-powered health advice, ensuring you’re never alone in a medical emergency.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, AI-driven healthcare solutions that empower individuals to make informed decisions during medical emergencies.

Our Vision

To create a world where healthcare is instant, reliable, and accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Our Impact

Helped over 10,000+ users find nearby hospitals, learn first aid, and receive AI-driven health advice in critical moments.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SwiftTiba was born out of a passion to bridge the gap between individuals and immediate healthcare access. Our team of healthcare professionals and AI experts came together to create a platform that leverages technology to save lives.

From locating the nearest hospital to providing step-by-step first aid guides, SwiftTiba is your trusted companion in every health-related scenario.
