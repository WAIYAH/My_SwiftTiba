Common Emergencies

Quick access to life-saving first aid instructions for the most critical situations

CPR

Critical

Cardiopulmonary resuscitation for cardiac arrest. Check responsiveness, call for help, begin chest compressions.

Choking

Critical

Heimlich maneuver and back blows for airway obstruction. Act fast â€” brain damage in 4 minutes without oxygen.

Severe Bleeding

Critical

Apply direct pressure, elevate the wound, use a tourniquet if necessary. Don't remove soaked bandages.

Burns

Urgent

Cool with running water for 10-15 minutes. Remove tight items near the burn. Cover with sterile dressing.

Fractures

Urgent

Don't move the injured area. Immobilize with a splint or sling. Apply ice and elevate when possible.
