Our Journey

The SwiftTiba Story

How a vision to save lives became Africa's trusted emergency care platform.

Founded in 2023, SwiftTiba was born from a simple but powerful observation: too many people in Kenya and across Africa lack immediate access to critical healthcare information during emergencies.

"Tiba" means "cure" or "treatment" in Swahili. Combined with "Swift," our name embodies our core promise â€” fast, accessible healthcare when seconds count.

Starting as a small project by passionate healthcare advocates and technologists, SwiftTiba has grown into a comprehensive digital health platform serving thousands of users. Our journey began with a mobile-first first aid guide and has expanded to include AI-powered symptom checking, a hospital locator covering Nairobi's major healthcare facilities, and a health education blog.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand, driven by our unwavering commitment to the belief that access to healthcare information should be a right, not a privilege.
